RubiCon (Rubisco Condensation) was designed to analyze and quantify phase separation in LAMMPS molecular dynamics simulations of Rubisco and linker proteins. It provides a robust pipeline for detecting condensates, Calculating phase-specific properties (concentration, volume and analyzing dynamics (diffusion).
